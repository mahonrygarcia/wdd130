Half Day Splash (Class II)

Perfect for families and beginners. Enjoy a scenic 3-hour float down the crystal clear upper canyon with plenty of swimming spots and gorgeous vistas.

Big Mallard Run (Class III)

Our most popular journey! A full-day excursion featuring rolling waves, exciting drops, and a riverside lunch prepared fresh by your guides.

The Devil's Teeth (Class IV)

Strictly for thrill-seekers. Fast-paced, heavy water action through narrow channels. Previous rafting experience is highly recommended for this intense expedition.
